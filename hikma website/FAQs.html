Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of skincare products, including face masks, serums, cleansers, moisturizers, and more, all designed to help you achieve glowing skin.

2. How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Orders typically take 2-3 business days to process and ship. Delivery time depends on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout.

3. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on the destination country.

4. How can I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact our customer support team via email at support@glowyshop.com or through our contact page.

5. Can I return a product?

Yes, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Please visit our Return Policy page for more details on how to return a product.
